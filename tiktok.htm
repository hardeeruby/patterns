TUTORIAL: BASEBALL CAP

The Crown (Top Part of Hat)
Start the circle:
Make a magic ring OR chain 4 and slip stitch into the first chain to form a circle.
Round 1: Chain 2 (counts as first stitch). Make 12 DC into the circle. Join with a slip stitch.
Round 2: Chain 2. Do 2 double crochets in the first stitch, then 1 double crochet in the next stitch. Repeat this pattern (2 dc, 1 dc) all the way around. Join with a slip stitch.

Round 3: Chain 2. (2 dc, 2 dc) around.
Join with a slip stitch. (24 stitches total)
Round 4: Chain 2. Inc, 3dc). Join with a slip stitch. (30 stitches total)
Round 5: Chain 2.
Do 2 double crochets in the first stitch, then 1 double crochet in each of the next 4 sts.
Repeat around.
Join with a slip stitch. (36 stitches total)
Rounds 6–15: Chain 2.
Do 1 double crochet in each stitch around.
Join with a slip stitch then chain 1 after each round.

The Brim
1. Chain 20.
Row 1–2: Double crochet across
3. Row 3: Double crochet 7, half double crochet 1, single crochet 1. Chain 1, turn.
4. Row 4:
Decrease, half double crochet 1, then double crochet 6. Chain 2, turn.
5. Row 5: Double crochet 4, half double crochet 1, single crochet 1. Chain 1, turn. 6.
Row 6: Decrease, half double crochet 1, then double crochet 3. Chain 2, turn.
7. Row 7: Double crochet 2, half double crochet 1, single crochet 1. Chain 1, turn.
8. Row 8: Decrease, half double crochet 1, double crochet 1.
Chain 2, turn.
Row 9: Double crochet 1, half double crochet 1.
Chain 1, turn. 10.
Row 10: Decrease, then double crochet 1.
Chain 2, turn.
Row 11: Double crochet 2. Repeat the same shaping on the other side of the brim. Then cut your yarn.

Finishing the Bottom 1. Make a slip knot into the 3rd stitch at the bottom of the brim. Ch 2
Double crochet across until 3 stitches are left.
Decrease in the next stitch. Chain 2, skip the first stitch, double crochet across until 2 stitches are left, then decrease.
Chain 2, skip the first 2 stitches, single crochet across until 3 stitches are left, then decrease.



DIAMONDBACKS

Crochet Fitted Hat New Era

Top of Hat
1. Make a magic ring (or chain 4, join into a circle).
2. Chain 2, make 12 double crochets into the circle. Join.
3. Chain 2, 2 double crochets in 1 stitch, 1 double crochet in the next. Repeat around. Join.
4. Chain 2, 2 double crochets in 1 stitch, then 1 double crochet in the next 2 stitches. Repeat. Join.
5. Chain 2, 2 double crochets in 1 stitch, then 1 double crochet in the next 3 stitches. Repeat. Join.
6. Chain 2, 2 double crochets in 1 stitch, then 1 double crochet in the next 4 stitches. Repeat. Join.
7. Chain 2, double crochet in every stitch around. Do this for 10 more rounds (Rounds 6-15).

Brim
1. Chain 22
2. Rows 1-2: Double crochet across.
Row 3: 7 double crochet, 1 half double crochet, 1 single crochet. Turn.
4. Row 4: Decrease, 1 half double crochet, 6 double crochet. Turn.
5. Row 5: 4 double crochet, 1 half double crochet, 1 single crochet. Turn.
6. Row 6: Decrease, 1 half double crochet, 3 double crochet. Turn.
7. Row 7:2 double crochet, 1 half double crochet, 1 single crochet. Turn.
8. Row 8: Decrease, 1 half double crochet, 1 double crochet. Turn.
9. Row 9: 1 double crochet, 1 half double crochet. Turn.
10. Row 10: Decrease, 1 double crochet. Turn.
11. Row 11: 2 double crochet. - Repeat the same rows for the other side of the brim. Cut yarn.

Repeat the same rows for the other side of the brim. Cut yarn.

Bottom Edge
1. Attach yarn into the 3rd stitch at the bottom.
2. Chain 2, double crochet across until
3 stitches are left, then decrease.
3. Chain 2, skip the first stitch, double crochet across until 2 stitches are left, then decrease.
4. Chain 2, skip the first 2 stitches, single crochet across until 3 stitches are left, then decrease.
